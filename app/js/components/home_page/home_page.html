Carlie's Trip to Africa

Hey there. My name is Carlie. I currently live in Chicago and I decided to make t-shirts! I have always wanted to design a shirt and now I have a reason too—I am going to Africa!

Your Support Matters

I have always had a heart for Africa, even when I was a young kid. Before going to college, I remember I just wanted to own an orphanage in Africa. Now I am finally moving towards that dream! This summer I get a chance to be in Mozambique, with the orphans and marginalized, getting to teach them, learn from them, and grow with them. I desire the people there to experience the love of God and be changed forever. My goal is to be in Africa long term, so this summer is a stepping stone into something more concrete. The shirts are a way to support me getting there!

Bringing Joy and Freedom

Product with Purpose

The meaning behind the shirt is that God gave me a dream to go to Africa and I said, “yes”! The front of the shirt says “Wherever you go…” and the back, “I will go…” because I am going with God where He wants me to go. When you guys support me, it’s like a piece of you is going with me too, by investing in me! The shirt is a tangible reminder of the people in Africa you are investing in with me. Thank you guys for supporting!! Enjoy the shirt!
